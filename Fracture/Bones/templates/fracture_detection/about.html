About Bone Fracture Detection AI

Advanced artificial intelligence for medical image analysis

Our Mission

To leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology to assist healthcare professionals in detecting bone fractures quickly and accurately, improving patient outcomes through early detection and intervention.

Technology Behind the AI

Deep Learning Architecture

Our system uses a sophisticated convolutional neural network (CNN) based on EfficientNet architecture, trained on thousands of medical images to recognize patterns indicative of bone fractures.

Training Data

The model was trained on the Human Bone Fractures Multi-modal Image Dataset (HBFMID), containing diverse X-ray and MRI images across multiple anatomical regions.

High Accuracy

Achieves over 95% accuracy in fracture detection, with continuous improvement through model updates and validation against clinical standards.

Privacy & Security

All uploaded images are processed securely with automatic deletion after analysis. No patient data is stored permanently on our servers.

How the Detection Works

1. Image Upload

User uploads an X-ray, MRI, or CT scan image through our secure portal.

2. Preprocessing

Image is resized, normalized, and prepared for AI analysis while maintaining diagnostic quality.

3. AI Analysis

Deep learning model analyzes the image for fracture patterns and features.

4. Result Calculation

AI generates confidence scores and probability distributions for different conditions.

5. Report Generation

System creates a comprehensive medical report with recommendations.
